About
Neko & Kopi Catfé

Welcome to Neko & Kopi Catfé, where the enchanting world of Studio Ghibli meets the comfort of a cozy café.
Nestled in the heart of Nawala, Sri Lanka, our space is a haven for anime enthusiasts and cat lovers alike. Inspired by the whimsical charm of Ghibli films,
we've created an atmosphere filled with delightful décor, themed treats, and the soothing presence of our resident cats. Whether you're here to sip on a Totoro latte, indulge in a Spirited Away-inspired dessert, or simply bask in the magical ambiance, we invite you to be part of our ever-growing community. Come, experience the magic!
